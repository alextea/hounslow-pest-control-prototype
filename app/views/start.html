Before you start

We deal with a wide range of pest species including

  • Rats
  • Mice
  • Squirrels
  • Fleas
  • Cockroaches
  • Bedbugs
  • Pharaoh ants
  • Garden ants
  • Wasps
  • Pigeons nesting in loft, etc

Pests we do not treat:

  • Foxes
  • Wood boring insects
  • Bees

Charges

For all fixed-rate services payment is required in advance. Cash or cheque is no longer accepted, we only accept card payments. Payments must be made at over the phone at the point of booking

Pest Control Services are non-statutory and do not have to be provided by the Local Authority. The service must now cover the full costs of provision and, as a result, we can no longer offer concessionary rates for householders in receipt of certain benefits.
